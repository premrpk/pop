About you:

You must have the highest regard for safety and security. A key standout from you career history will be your ability to maintain the highest professional standards and provide excellent team based customer service and support in a fast paced and ever changing work environment. Your willingness and availability to work across a 7 day a week, shift patterned style roster will be essential.



Job Responsibilities:

Ensure the safety and security of passengers on board by following established protocols and procedures.
Provide excellent customer service, anticipating and exceeding the needs and expectations of passengers.
Demonstrate thorough knowledge of safety equipment, emergency procedures, and first aid techniques.
Communicate effectively with the flight crew, ground staff, and other team members to ensure seamless operations.
Handle passenger enquiries, resolve issues, and address any concerns with professionalism and empathy.
Stay up to date about industry regulations and company policies, always maintaining compliance.

Fluent in English.
Australian Citizen or Permanent Resident (New Zealand Passport Holders Included)

Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
Strong problem-solving abilities and adaptability to changing situations.

Previous customer service experience essential
